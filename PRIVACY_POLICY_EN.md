# SimpliTask Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** April 15, 2026

## Introduction

SimpliTask ("we", "our", or "the app") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our task management application.

## Information We Collect

### What We Don't Collect
- **Personal Identification**: We do not collect names, emails, phone numbers
- **Network Data**: The app works completely offline, no network servers
- **Location Data**: We do not access your geographic location
- **Device Information**: We do not upload device identifiers

### What We Store
All data is **stored locally on your device only**:
- Task names and descriptions you create
- Task categories and priority settings
- Task schedules and completion status
- App preferences (such as language settings)

## Data Storage

- **Local Storage**: All data is saved using Hive database on your device
- **No Cloud Sync**: Data is not uploaded to any server
- **No Third-Party Access**: No third parties can access your data

## Permissions

Permissions requested by this app:
- **No Special Permissions**: No network, location, camera, contacts, or other sensitive permissions required

## Data Security

- Data exists only in your device's local storage
- All data will be deleted when the app is uninstalled
- We do not share your data with any third parties

## Children's Privacy

This app is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated policy will be displayed within the app.
